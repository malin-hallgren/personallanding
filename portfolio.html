Hearts of Iron IV:

Götterdämmerung

My work on Austria consisted of... well... everything regarding Austria really. The entire release cycle of an expansion starts with a expansive research phase that then informs the design throughout. This then moved into an intensive phase of implementation of the design and iteration upon it as feedback came in. I collaborated closely with designers on related countries (Germany and Hungary) as well as QA to ensure the content was always devloping in the expected direction while still being balanced and fun to interact with, be it as a player of the Austrian content, or as a player of any other country interacting with the Austrian AI.

With the responsibility of Austria as a country comes several critical systems such as the so called Anschluss which kicks off, or fails to kick off, the entire game. For more information on individual systems I will recommend a read through the Developer Diary I wrote as release drew near.

Beyond Austria this expansion also saw me become one of the technical "go-to" people when faced with particularly stubborn bugs, and as such I also took custody over several critical and high maintenance systems making use of inhouse systems with less than ideal documentation.

Check out the expansion on Steam.
